I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich, in Joseph Ayoub's research group. Before that I was a PhD student at the University of Duisburg-Essen in Marc Levine's group. I am interested in interactions between algebraic geometry and homotopy theory.

More specifically I work in 'A^1-homotopy theory' which allows one to consider 'motivic spectra', a category of a homotopic nature encompassing all (A^1-invariant, Nisnevich local) cohomology theories on smmoth schemes.
In my PhD research I consider invariants for schemes defined using the machinery of this category, mostly lying in the Grothendieck-Witt ring of the base field. Those refine integer-valued enumerative geometry invarinats. I use them to gather information about singularities of schemes. Here is a link to a preprint based on my PhD thesis
